Mahara-Match AI is an advanced, context-aware talent acquisition intelligence platform designed to move past the limitations of legacy keyword-matching ATS systems. Operating as an AI co-pilot for talent acquisition teams, it maps complex human capital profiles against raw job constraints through semantic relationships, multi-agent evaluation workflows, and deeply ethical scoring filters.

Version 4.0 shifts the Proof of Concept (PoC) into an institutional-grade pipeline by introducing an explicit **Ethics & Bias Agent**, a high-density Knowledge Graph expansion, hybrid lexical/vector search mechanics, and automated localized dynamic upskilling paths.

---

## 🎯 Strategic Value for Technical Recruiters

Traditional Applicant Tracking Systems (ATS) accidentally eliminate exceptional candidates who happen to omit precise buzzwords. Mahara-Match AI completely changes the sourcing and evaluation paradigm:

* **Context Over Keywords:** Powered by a **120+ node structural Knowledge Graph**, the pipeline evaluates directional and inverse competencies (e.g., automatically inferring a candidate with a deep "TensorFlow Lite" background natively understands embedded model optimization).
* **Active Equity & Bias Mitigation:** Includes an active **Ethics & Bias Detection Agent** that systematically audits resumes and job listings for unconscious gender, age, or regional biases, helping your team scale diverse and compliant hiring loops.
* **Explainable 5D Scoring Matrix:** Delivers transparent, vector-decomposed evaluations across **Technical Skills, Soft Skills, Experience Level, Cultural Fit, and Potential** alongside an LLM confidence index. No more unexplained percentages.
* **Local Upskilling Integrations:** If an exceptional candidate falls slightly short on specific technical criteria, the system cross-references localized vocational frameworks (such as the **ATFP** and **OFPPT** catalogs) to build instant training recommendations into their onboarding plan.
